Upgrades!

The Quiz component has reciveved an upgrade with new challenging question types. You can still use your multiple choice questions and next to that you can now use group, fill in the blanks and form a sentence formats!

Special syntax

There is a special syntax you can use with double brackets to indicate that that [[word]] of [[multiple words]] are one part of a sentence. You can also add additional words to make the question harder to answer.

Some quizes are now marked Outdated to indicate that they are still in the old format.
